Images

Let’s have some fun with images!!!

JPG

They are good with color pallettes and for high gradient pics, but they don’t have transparent pixels.

GIF

They have a limited color palette, best for simple animation. They have transparent pixels but its set with a binary option therefore it doesnt support semi opaque pixels.

PNG

PNG are best suited for small images like logos, good with opacity and have no pallette limitations.

SVG

SVGs are amazing for just about everything. They use vector-based graphics
